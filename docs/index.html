WikiReveal Reveal the Wikipedia Page Behind the Redacted Words

WikiReveal is a daily browser game where the player have to determine the subject of a random obfuscated Wikipedia article.
A new puzzle will be available every day at 3:00 AM UTC.

Available languages

  • English: see Redactle.com
  • Français
  • Add your language by contributing

How to Play

To win, you have to figure out the title of the article.
Type a word into the input box and press Enter or the button. All occurrences of that word will be revealed in the article.

Tips and Quirks

  • Each guess must be one word only (multiple words will not work)
  • Guesses are case-insensitive, and letters with diacritics (é, ü, etc) are considered to not have those diacritics
  • Some glitches can be present, usually they don't have mush incidence on the game
  • Donations to Wikipedia are encouraged

Inspiration & contributions

This game is inspired by Redactle.com.
